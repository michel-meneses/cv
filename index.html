Who am I?

I am a machine learning engineer at SiDi (Samsung R&D Institute), where I perform cutting-edge research on deep learning for on-device speech processing in Samsung Galaxy mobile devices, such as the camera voice control and Bixby hands-free wake-up. I also manage TensorFlow Lite - The Professional Course, a digital course I co-created that instructs ML engineers and software developers on how to embed, evaluate and optimize ML models for running on edge devices. Previously, I completed my M.S. in Computer Science and my B.E. in Computer Engineering at the Federal University of Sergipe. In both cases, I explored AI algorithms for real-time computer vision.

I am currently interested in abstracting the fundamentals of biological intelligence into more effective AI systems.
